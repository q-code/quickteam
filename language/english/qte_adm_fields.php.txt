This screen allows you defining the information accessible in the user profiles (and in the team listing) and to rename/translate it.<br /><b>Uncheck</b> the info that you don't need to manage. To define the information you want to display in the team listing, see the menu 'Manage teams'. You can define a specific php <b>format</b>. When allowed, you can also use the format to define a drop-down list of values.
